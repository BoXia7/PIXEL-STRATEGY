# Tokenomics

## Fee Structure

Pixel Strategy is designed to capture 100% of creator fees received from pump.fun token launches. These fees, typically paid in SOL, are automatically collected and reinvested into strategic digital and pixelated assets.

### Creator Fees
- **Source**: pump.fun platform fees for token creation
- **Allocation**: 100% reinvested
- **Purpose**: Building a sustainable reserve of digital assets

## Reserve Management

The project's reserve is managed through a dedicated Solana program that:
- Holds collected fees securely on-chain
- Tracks all reinvestments transparently
- Provides real-time visibility through the dashboard

## Value Proposition

By reinvesting all creator fees into assets rather than distributing them, Pixel Strategy creates a compounding effect that increases the project's long-term value and sustainability.

## Transparency

All fee collections and reinvestments are recorded on-chain, ensuring complete transparency for the community.
